Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a comprehensive hub for showcasing my professional portfolio and providing detailed information about my skills, experience, and services. Visitors will find a home page introducing myself, a projects page highlighting my completed work with descriptions and links to live demos or repositories, and a contact page for inquiries and collaboration opportunities. The site aims to cater to potential employers, clients, and collaborators by offering an organized and visually appealing presentation of my expertise.

Audience

This website is designed for potential employers, clients, and collaborators seeking to learn about my work and expertise. It also welcomes anyone with an interest in web development and design.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

What are the key skills and technologies Jorge specializes in, and how can they benefit my project?

I specialize in front-end and back-end web development, with expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Node.js. My skills ensure that your project will have a modern, responsive design and efficient functionality tailored to your needs.

Scenario 2

Where can I find examples of Jorge's previous work, and how can I contact him for collaboration or hiring?

You can find examples of my previous work on the Projects page, which includes detailed descriptions, live demos, and links to repositories. For collaboration or hiring inquiries, you can visit the Contact page to send me a message directly.
